Welcome to MtG DraftSim!

How to Play

DraftSim takes a given collection of cards and allows you to simulate draft and sealed games with it. To play, first create or import a collection. Next, select the format you wish to simulate.

Using the Collection Builder

The Collection Builder helps you create the starting card pool. You can search for existing cards by name, filtering the results by set, rarity, color, and more! Just click a card to add it to the collection (up to 4 copies). Once you have enough cards in the collection, you're ready to play!

Playing a Sealed Game

The sealed format simulates opening 6 booster packs from your collection. To play this mode, you must have at least: 6 mythic rares, 6 rares, 18 uncommon cards, and 60 common cards.
